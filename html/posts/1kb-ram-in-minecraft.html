1KB of RAM in Minecraft

2022-09-05

In this post, I will describe how to make 1024KB of RAM in Minecraft out of 1024 * 8 (8196) edge-triggered D-type flip-flops. With 1024KB of RAM, we will be able to store 1024 1-byte values (or 8196 bits). Or 0000h through 03FFh in hexadecimal (0 through 1024 in decimal). If you would like to learn how to make an 8x1 RAM to store any 8-bit value, checkout this post. To do this, I will apply everything I've learned from reading chapter sixteen of "Code: The Hidden Language of Computer Hardware and Software" by Charles Petzold.
